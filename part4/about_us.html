Our Story

Founded in 2024, HBNB started with a simple idea: to make travel more personal and accessible. We believe that where you stay should be as memorable as where you go.

Today, we've grown into a global community of hosts and travelers, offering unique accommodations in over 100 countries.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to create a world where anyone can belong anywhere. Through our platform, we connect people who want to share their homes with those looking for unique places to stay.
